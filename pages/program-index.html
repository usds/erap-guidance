Treasury is providing an index of all Emergency Rental Assistance Programs below to make it easier for tenants and landlords to find local assistance. We encourage you to seek assistance from the local resource closest to you, when available, before seeking assistance from your state as your city and county may have funds available specifically for your location.

Find programs in...

  • U.S. States
  • U.S. Territories
  • Tribes and Tribally Designated Housing Entities (TDHEs)

Note that the list below does contain some rental assistance programs that are currently operating in that location but are not specifically programs funded by Treasury. These links are provided in order to help tenants and landlords find the most immediately accessible assistance.

If you are a grantee and feel that the information on this page is incorrect, please send updates to PLACEHOLDER@treasury.gov.
