The Treasury has collected links associated with the various Emergency Rental Assistance programs to help tenants and landlords find rental assistance in your area. These links may include resources funded outside of the ERA program.

Each grantee has some flexibility to develop their rental assistance program to suit the needs of their local community. While grantees have agreed to follow certain guidelines, individual requirements and options may vary. These links may include resources funded outside of the ERA program.

Grantees are encouraged to notify Treasury of updates to ERA tenant/landlord access information.

Helpful tip

Check the list to see if your state, county, or Tribe/Tribal Affiliation has a more specific URL before applying through the state. In most cases, states encourage tenants and landlords to apply to the most local program available.
